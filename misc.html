This is an example of work that didn't fit into any of the categories above.

This study of a display of lilies was painted on a canvas given to me.

It had previously been painted as a very loose abstract with a small amount of texture in the paint.

This I scraped off and initially, had intended to cover the surface during the painting of the lilies.

However, as I worked I realised that the green echoed the colour of the leaves seen through the glass of the vase and the brown provided a little warmth into what otherwise might have been too cool a painting.

I hope you will agree with me that I made the right decision.
