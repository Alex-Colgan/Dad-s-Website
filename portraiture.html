This self portrait was painted in couple of afternoons with a lamp to my right and natural light to my left.

The combination of the two light sources provided some interesting contrasts across the face.

It was painted, like the majority of my work, with acrylics on the smooth side of a sheet of hardboard prepared with several coats of white acrylic undercoat.

My children have been somewhat critical of the result, commenting that I have made myself appear too 'grumpy' quite unlike my normal cheerful demeanour!
